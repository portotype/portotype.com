### How I invest

I want to invest in software and hard that helps users be the hero.

Technically, I hold stock in startups via investments and agreements, sometimes both. 
- Investments are situations where I directly invested money. I give $5k-$30k cash, I get shares.
- Scouting rewards me for helping a VC make good investments. I have advised for Accel Starters, Shilling Scouting and Invictus Capital.
- Being an Advisor rewards me with shares for being a Board Member or help in other forms, likely on top of a role or investment.
